Bio

My name is Hamzah Wilson born on July 30th, 1988, in a bustling city in China called Changsha. Growing up in Changsha was an adventure; the streets were always bustling with people, and the food was always delicious. I spent my early childhood exploring the city and making friends with other kids in the neighborhood. When I was seven years old, my family decided to move to Australia, and I left behind my childhood home and all my memories in Changsha. I was excited but nervous about this new chapter in my life. Everything was new and unfamiliar, but I soon fell in love with Australia, with its beautiful beaches, friendly people, and diverse culture. Over the years, I embraced the Australian way of life, making new friends, learning the language, and experiencing new things. However, even as I built a new life in Australia, I never forgot my roots and the city where I was born, Changsha.

ChangSha

My favourite place to visit will still always be ChangSha my birthplace, as it is embedded with memories of my childhood that I cannot forget. Changsha is a city located in central China, the capital of Hunan Province. It has a population of over 7 million people, making it one of the largest cities in China. Changsha has a long history and rich cultural heritage. The city is also known for its spicy cuisine, especially the local specialty of Hunan cuisine.
