Games

I have always been a gamer since I was a child. I started with games such as Mario, Doom and other similar games of the 90s, but when World of Warcraft came out, I was addicted. I loved WOW. It was a full-time job as I was part of the second-best guild in Oceania at the time. It meant that I had to be on top of my game, gathering resources and preparations for raids. All that being said, the friends and memories I have made could not be replaced by anything.

Nowadays, I tend to take the chiller side of gaming. As being in a constant battle with time, I have the issue of being able to sit down to enjoy a nice game, but I have found solace in a new type of game called Idle gaming and the king of them all is Idleon. It has all the RPG elements I love without having to spend hours a day managing resources and opening Excel sheets on improvements. I hope I can eventually make my contributions to the world of gaming.
